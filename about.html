About Me

Hi, I'm Dehn. Scroll down to get to know me better!

Hi, I’m Dehn See Toh, currently a third-year undergraduate from NUS, majoring in Finance. I've studied in Singapore pretty much my entire life, I graduated from Raffles Institution's six-year IP in 2012. I'm currently taking the module IT1001: Introduction to Computing which has compelled me to create and publish this website online.

When I’m not in school, I’m usually following the financial markets, reading about the latest technology trends, playing basketball or tennis, gaming, browsing reddit or hanging out with friends and family.

In my posts, I’ll primarily be sharing with you my reviews of some of the games I play in my free time. The reason I choose to do this rather than dissect the latest developments in the financial markets is that there are other resources on the web more qualified than me who are readily able and willing to share their opinion, whereas I would like to spend what little time I have to edit this site discussing what would be the most interesting to everyone.

I generally prefer competitive multi-player team games such as Dota 2 and Overwatch; I find the thrill of competing and succeeding as a team far outweighs the satisfaction I get from any single-player game. However, I have dabbled in single-player games in the past like Skyrim, Planet Coaster and the tycoon family of games.
